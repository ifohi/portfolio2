hello!

Growing up, I drew my favorite cartoon characters or some still life objects, and then polished it off by meticulously coloring them. Afterward, I proudly hung them up on my bedroom wall. My drawings didn’t stay up long, though, because as I passed by them, I wasn’t satisfied and saw ways to improve them.

Even at a young age, I tweaked my work and developed a habit of re-doing something until I’m satisfied (at least, for that moment). I continue to flex these muscles as a UX/UI designer, except now I’m iterating again and again not to necessarily satisfy myself but for the user. During my projects, I test for user feedback and changes are based on data. Come and check out my latest case study, a cloud-based platform where users can easily organize and share their contents: Store.it.
